# What is measured?
We take the Carbon Dioxide equivalent (CO<sub>2</sub>-eq) has measurement. It takes into account the collective > > > contribution of greenhouse gases (including CO<sub>2</sub>, CH<sub>4</sub>, NO<sub>2</sub>, etc.)

### LCA: Life Cycle Assessment
Building life cycle is divided following 3 stages
- Construction stage: Material production, material transportation, construction on site
- Operational stage: HVAC (heating, ventilation, air conditioning), lighting, water supply, equipment use, refurbishment. Great variability following climatic conditions, comfort requirements, operating schedules
- Demolition stage: demolition and refurbishment
### BLCCE general formula
$$ C_{tot}=C_{con}+C_{ope}+C_{dem} $$
with $C_{tot}, C_{con}, C_{ope}, C_{dem}$ respectively as total, construction, operating and demolition stages carbon emissions.
